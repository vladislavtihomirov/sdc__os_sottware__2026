# SSE Task 26: Ideal Final Result

## Return to the pair of requirement contradiction

**System:** Airport luggage system  
**Object:** Conveyor system

---

## Pair of requirement contradiction

| Requirement | Description |
|---|---|
| Requirement 1 | The conveyor system must transport many bags quickly to increase baggage throughput. |
| Requirement 2 | The conveyor system must prevent jams, poor spacing, misrouting, and luggage damage. |

---

## Undesirable effect

High conveyor speed and high baggage flow can cause **bag jams, poor spacing, misrouting, and luggage damage**.

---

## Ideal Final Result definition

The **airport luggage system itself** creates conditions for implementing **fast and high-volume luggage transportation**,  

and for preventing the appearing of **bag jams, poor spacing, misrouting, and luggage damage**.
