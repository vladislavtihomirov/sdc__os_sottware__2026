# SSE Task 24: Operation Time

## Return to the contradiction in property

**System:** Airport luggage system  
**Object:** Conveyor system  
**Property:** Speed of luggage movement

---

## Contradiction in property

The **speed of luggage movement** of the **conveyor system** must be **high** to transport many bags quickly and increase baggage throughput.

The **speed of luggage movement** of the **conveyor system** must be **low / controlled** to prevent jams, poor spacing, misrouting, and luggage damage.

---

## Time periods for value 1 and value 2

| Property value | Time period | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Value 1: High speed** | During normal baggage flow, when bags are evenly spaced and no congestion is detected | Increase throughput and move many bags quickly |
| **Value 2: Low / controlled speed** | During merging, scanning, sorting, congestion, or exception handling | Improve reliability, spacing, scanning accuracy, and safety |

---

## Type of intersection for time periods

**Type of intersection:** Partial intersection

The two time periods partly overlap because the system may need high throughput and controlled movement at the same time, especially near scanners, diverters, and merge points.
