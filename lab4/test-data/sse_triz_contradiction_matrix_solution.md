# SSE Task: TRIZ Contradiction Matrix Application

## Selected contradiction

**System:** Airport luggage system

**Contradiction:**  
The conveyor system must transport many bags quickly, but high speed and high load reduce reliability because they can cause jams, poor spacing, and misrouting.

---

## TRIZ matrix parameters

| Requirement | Selected TRIZ parameter |
|---|---|
| Positive effect: increase baggage throughput | **39. Productivity** |
| Negative effect: reduce system reliability | **27. Reliability** |

---

## Matrix result

For the contradiction:

- **String / row:** 39. Productivity
- **Column:** 27. Reliability

The selected TRIZ inventive principles are:

1. **Principle 10: Preliminary action**
2. **Principle 18: Mechanical vibration**

---

## Applying TRIZ principles

| TRIZ principle | Meaning | Solution idea for airport luggage system |
|---|---|---|
| **10. Preliminary action** | Prepare the required action before it is needed | Pre-sort bags at bag drop by flight, priority, and transfer status before they enter the main conveyor |
| **10. Preliminary action** | Arrange objects in advance to reduce delay | Assign bags to conveyor time slots before movement so they enter the belt with correct spacing |
| **18. Mechanical vibration** | Use vibration or oscillation to support the process | Add gentle vibration modules near merge points to separate bags and prevent clustering |
| **18. Mechanical vibration** | Use controlled vibration to distribute or align objects | Use vibrating guide plates before scanners to align bags and improve tag reading |

---

## Generated solution ideas

1. **Pre-routing at bag drop**  
   Each bag receives a route before entering the conveyor.

2. **Controlled entry gates**  
   Bags are released onto the conveyor in timed intervals.

3. **Smart spacing system**  
   Sensors detect bag distance and pause entry if bags are too close.

4. **Vibrating alignment zones**  
   Gentle vibration aligns bags before scanners and diverters.

5. **Separate priority lanes**  
   Transfer bags and short-connection bags use a faster controlled lane.
