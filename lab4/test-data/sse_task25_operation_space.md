# SSE Task 25: Operation Space

## Return to the contradiction in property

**System:** Airport luggage system  
**Object:** Conveyor system  
**Property:** Speed of luggage movement

---

## Contradiction in property

The **speed of luggage movement** of the **conveyor system** must be **high** to transport many bags quickly and increase baggage throughput.

The **speed of luggage movement** of the **conveyor system** must be **low / controlled** to prevent jams, poor spacing, misrouting, and luggage damage.

---

## Operation spaces for value 1 and value 2

| Property value | Operation space | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Value 1: High speed** | Long straight conveyor sections with stable bag flow | Move many bags quickly and increase throughput |
| **Value 2: Low / controlled speed** | Merge points, scanners, diverters, sorting zones, and loading area entrances | Improve bag spacing, identification, sorting accuracy, and safety |

---

## Type of intersection for operation spaces

**Type of intersection:** Partial intersection

The operation spaces partly intersect because some conveyor zones need both high throughput and controlled movement, especially before scanners and sorting diverters.
